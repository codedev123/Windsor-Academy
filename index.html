Here are some of our latest clubs.

Football Club

Here at our Football Club we are offering kids aged 11 till 17 to join and we will support their potential football careers the best we can referring them to higher division football teams and even academies.We will regularly set friendly matches agaisnt other schools and even host tournaments.We will have training on both Tuesday and Thursday afterschool from 3:10pm to 4:10pm.

Literacy Club

Here at our Literacy Club we offer a multitude of skills and knowledge to kids ages 11-17 that will benefit your child in the future.Here at our Literacy Club we offer: poetry, reading, storytelling, story design, creative writing, board games and a bunch of other literature related content that will boost your child up in our literacy inbued world.we host our Literacy Club on Mondays and Wednesdays 3:10 to 4:10.

Lego Club

Here at our Lego Club we offer a multitude of skills and knowledge to children ages 11-14 that will benefit your child in the future.Here at our Lego Club we offer a newfound method of igniting curiosity and ingenuity that will eventually lead on to coding,computer sciences and much more espacially in our forever changing world where computers will be a part of our society.We host Lego Club Thursday and Friday 3:10 to 4:10.
